Miles Davis

irth name Miles Dewey Davis III Born May 26, 1926 Alton, Illinois, U.S. Died September 28, 1991 (aged 65) Santa Monica, California, U.S. Genres Jazzfusion Occupation(s) Musicianbandleadercomposer Instrument(s) Trumpet, flugel horn, cornet, piano, electric organ

Born into an upper-middle-class family in Alton, Illinois, and raised in East St. Louis, Davis started on the trumpet in his early teens. He left to study at Juilliard in New York City, before dropping out and making his professional debut as a member of saxophonist Charlie Parker's bebop quintet from 1944 to 1948.
